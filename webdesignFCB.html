La Remuntada: The Treble is Coming

FIRST TEAM PLAYERS
View all Players

Marc Andre Ter Stergen, Yes, he's human, and yes, he's a barca player, the german shot-stopper has been a bane in the existence of opponents, the clog in the wheels of their strike force, their cloud of grey and everything in-between. The goal keeper whose prowess is one to be envied by professional contortionists, he makes it a point of duty to tumble out of bed each morning and deliver such spectacular performances on this grand theatre of the football field, his ability is unrivalled and he keeps on improving.

Our legend-Anu, confesses that the player reminds him of a squirrel, and he's right..however, whatever similarities he bears to a squirrel is limited to his facial features alone, his speed as they say, is in the eye of the beholder (most times the opposing right-back) toddlers have been known to throw banana skins and to squeal with delight at the sight of him peeling down the turf, his speed is a byword in these parts and he makes us all happy.
